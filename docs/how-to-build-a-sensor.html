2.2 Prerequisite

2.2.1 Hardware

Item Function Requirement
Laptop and LAN cable To access and control your sensor
Raspberry Pi board To build your sensor Pi 3B/3B+ or a higher model Pi
WiFi adapter To capture WiFi packets Check your chipset that supports ‘monitoring mode’ here
Micro SD card and adapter To build and store the data At least 16 GB size
Ethernet cable To connect the Pi with your laptop
Portable power bank To run the sensor in outdoor environment +20000 mAh battery
* It lasts one-day in our setting
Pi camera To record the scene in the front of sensor

✅ A laptop and lan cable to control your sensor
✅ A Raspberry Pi board to build your sensor
✅ A WiFi adapter supporting ‘monitoring mode’; please check your chipset here
✅ A micro SD card of at least 16GB size
✅ A portable power bank for sensing in outdoor environment that is difficult to supply power
- 20000 mAh portable battery lasts approximately one-day in our setting

✅ A Pi camera if you want to record the scene in the front of sensor
